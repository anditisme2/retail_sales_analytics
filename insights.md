# Observations obtained from dataset\
## Champions
1. Have the highest average monetary value
2. Purchase relatively recently and frequently, showing high engagement
3. Represent a small but highly valuable customer segment driving the largest share of revenue\
## Potential Loyalists
1. The largest customer segment, contributing significantly to total revenue due to sheer volume
2. Show moderate-to-high-spending but lower frequency than Champions
3. Exhibit strong potential to transition into high-value customers\
## At Risk
1. Generated meaningful revenue in the past but have been inactive recently (high recency values)
2. Show declining purchase frequency
3. Represent a critical churn-risk segment where revenue loss is likely\
## Low Value
1. Have the lowest average monetary value among all segments
2. Show low frequency and low spend, indicating minimal engagement
3. Contribute marginally to overall revenue and show limited growth potential
